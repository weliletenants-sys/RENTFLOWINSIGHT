## 1. General Questions

### Q: What is Welile?
**A:** Welile is a rent-guarantee and rent-facilitation platform that connects tenants, landlords, field agents, and supporters (funders/investors) in a trust-based financial ecosystem. Welile pays your landlord's rent upfront, and you repay in small daily installments over an agreed period. Welile does NOT lend money — it facilitates rent payments, manages risk, and earns service fees for infrastructure and guarantees.

### Q: Where does Welile operate?
**A:** Welile currently operates in Uganda with plans to expand across Africa and globally. The company is registered as Welile Technologies Limited and is based in Entebbe, Uganda.

### Q: Is Welile a bank or a lending institution?
**A:** No. Welile is a rent facilitation platform, not a bank or lender. The platform acts as a system operator and guarantor — connecting those who need rent paid with those who provide capital, and managing the entire process including verification, payments, collections, and risk.

### Q: Who can use Welile?
**A:** Anyone in Uganda who needs help paying rent (tenants), owns rental properties (landlords), wants to earn by facilitating connections (agents), or wants to invest capital and earn returns (supporters/funders). Users can hold multiple roles simultaneously.

### Q: How does Welile make money?
**A:** Welile earns revenue through:
- **Access fees** charged to tenants for rent facilitation (23%, 28%, or 33% monthly compounding rate)
- **Request fees** (UGX 10,000 for rent ≤ UGX 200,000; UGX 20,000 for rent > UGX 200,000)
- **Platform fees** on various financial transactions
- **Agent advance access fees** (33% compounding on agent cash advances)

## 2. Account & Registration

### Q: How do I create a Welile account?
**A:** You can register by:
1. **Self-registration** — Visit welile.com and sign up with your email and phone number.
2. **Agent-assisted registration** — A field agent can register you in person.
3. **Bulk import** — Partners and supporters can be onboarded by the operations team.

### Q: What information do I need to register?
**A:** You need:
- Full name
- Phone number (MTN or Airtel Uganda)
- Email address
- National ID or valid identification
- For tenants: Your landlord's details and property address
- For landlords: Property details and utility meter numbers

### Q: Can I have multiple roles on the platform?
**A:** Yes. A single user can simultaneously be a Tenant, Landlord, Agent, and Supporter. Each role unlocks a dedicated dashboard with role-specific features. You can switch between your dashboards seamlessly.

### Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
**A:** Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. A password reset link will be sent to your registered email address. If you were registered by an agent, your default password follows the format: `Welile` + last 6 digits of your phone number + `!` (e.g., phone 0700405936 → password `Welile405936!`).

### Q: How do I verify my email address?
**A:** After registration, a verification email is sent to your email address. Click the verification link in that email to activate your account. You must verify your email before you can sign in.

## 3. Tenant Questions

### Q: How does the rent facilitation process work?
**A:** The process follows these steps:
1. **Request** — You (or your agent) submit a rent request with your rent amount, landlord details, and property address.
2. **Verification** — A field agent visits your property, captures GPS coordinates, and verifies your landlord's identity.
3. **Approval** — A Welile manager reviews and approves the request.
4. **Payment** — Welile pays your landlord immediately from the Rent Management Pool.
5. **Repayment** — You repay in small daily installments over your chosen period (14–90 days).

### Q: How much does it cost to use Welile for rent?
**A:** Your total repayment includes:
- **Your actual rent amount** (paid directly to your landlord)
- **Access fee** — calculated using compounding at your chosen rate (23%, 28%, or 33% monthly)
- **Request fee** — UGX 10,000 (for rent ≤ UGX 200,000) or UGX 20,000 (for rent > UGX 200,000)

**Example:** Rent of UGX 200,000 for 30 days at 33% rate:
- Access Fee: UGX 66,000
- Request Fee: UGX 10,000
- Total: UGX 276,000
- Daily repayment: ~UGX 9,200/day

### Q: How do I repay?
**A:** Repayments happen through:
- **Auto-deduction** — Your wallet is charged automatically every day at 6:00 AM. Simply keep your wallet funded.
- **Agent collection** — A field agent can collect cash from you and credit your account.
- **Mobile Money deposit** — Deposit to your Welile wallet via MTN or Airtel, and the system auto-applies repayment.

### Q: What happens if I can't pay on a given day?
**A:** If your wallet has insufficient funds:
1. The system first tries to deduct whatever is available from your wallet.
2. The remaining balance is charged to your linked agent's wallet.
3. If the agent also has insufficient funds, the shortfall is recorded as accumulated debt.
4. When you next deposit money, all outstanding debt is cleared first before future installments.

### Q: What is the Tenant Agreement?
**A:** Before your rent is facilitated, you must accept the Welile Tenant Agreement. Key points:
- Welile holds tenant rights for the paid rent period; you remain as a house user.
- You agree to repay the total amount in daily installments via MTN/Airtel or approved channels.
- Payments made outside Welile channels may not be recognized.
- If you default, Welile may issue warnings, send field agents, and cooperate with the landlord and local leaders.

### Q: What happens if I deposit more money than my daily installment?
**A:** Excess funds are automatically applied to future installments. For example, if your daily charge is UGX 10,000 and you deposit UGX 50,000:
- Today's charge is paid (UGX 10,000)
- 4 future days are pre-paid (UGX 40,000)
- Your next charge date jumps forward 5 days

### Q: Can I choose my repayment period?
**A:** Yes. You can choose repayment periods of 14, 21, 30, 60, or 90 days. Shorter periods have lower total fees due to less compounding. Longer periods give you smaller daily amounts but higher total cost.

### Q: What is "Welile Homes"?
**A:** Welile Homes is a savings program where tenants can save towards homeownership. Your savings accumulate over time and are tracked separately from your rent repayments.

## 4. Agent Questions

### Q: What does an agent do?
**A:** Agents are Welile's field force. They:
- Register new tenants and landlords
- Verify properties and landlord identities using GPS
- Collect daily repayments from tenants in the field
- Deliver rent payments to landlords
- Manage a portfolio of tenants and landlords
- Can invest on behalf of supporter partners

### Q: How do agents earn money?
**A:** Agents earn through multiple channels:

| Activity | Earning |
|----------|---------|
| Tenant Registration | UGX 500 |
| Tenant Verification | UGX 5,000 |
| Rent Verification | UGX 5,000 |
| Approval Bonus (per approved request) | UGX 5,000 |
| Commission on Rent Repayments | 5% of every repayment collected |
| Sub-Agent Override | 1% passive income on sub-agent collections |
| Landlord Management Fee | 2% on rent payments for landlords without smartphones |

### Q: What is a sub-agent?
**A:** Sub-agents are agents recruited by a parent (super) agent. When a sub-agent collects repayments:
- The sub-agent earns 4% commission
- The parent super agent earns 1% passive override on every collection

### Q: What is the agent float system?
**A:** The float is the operating capital assigned to an agent for field collections and rent delivery. Agents have:
- A **float limit** set by management
- **Daily transaction limits**
- Real-time tracking of cash on hand vs. digital collections
- Float can be funded by managers and is reconciled daily

### Q: Can agents get cash advances?
**A:** Yes. Agents who qualify as Team Leaders (with 2+ sub-agents) can access cash advances from UGX 300,000 to UGX 30,000,000. Terms:
- 33% monthly compounding access fee
- Repayment periods: 7, 14, 30, 60, or 90 days
- Auto-deducted daily from agent wallet at 05:00 UTC
- Registration fee: UGX 10,000 (≤ 200k principal) or UGX 20,000 (> 200k principal)

### Q: What career rewards do agents get?
**A:** 
- **Team Leader status** — Access to cash advances when you recruit 2+ sub-agents
- **Electric Bike reward** — Earned at 50 repaying tenants
- **Collection streaks** — Badges and multiplier bonuses for consecutive collection days

### Q: How does the "Invest for Partner" feature work?
**A:** When an agent is in the field, they can invest on behalf of a supporter partner:
1. Tap "Invest for Partner"
2. Enter the partner's name, phone number, and investment amount
3. The system deducts from the agent's wallet
4. A portfolio is instantly activated for the partner
5. The partner's account is created (if new) with auto-generated credentials
6. The investment appears immediately in the Partner Ops dashboard

## 5. Landlord Questions

### Q: How do landlords get paid?
**A:** When a tenant's rent request is approved, the landlord receives payment through one of three channels:
1. **Landlord's Welile wallet** — If the landlord is registered on the platform
2. **Caretaker's wallet** — If the landlord has a designated caretaker
3. **Agent delivery** — The agent delivers cash to the landlord directly

### Q: What is the Guaranteed Rent Program?
**A:** The Guaranteed Rent Program ensures landlords receive their rent on time every month, regardless of tenant payment status. This is subject to verification by Welile managers and property eligibility criteria.

### Q: Do landlords pay any fees?
**A:** Landlords do not pay access fees or request fees. However, agents managing properties for landlords without smartphones earn a 2% management fee that is deducted from rent payments before crediting the landlord.

### Q: How does landlord verification work?
**A:** Landlords are verified through:
- Identity verification with unique PINs
- Property ownership documentation
- GPS-verified property location
- Utility meter number confirmation (electricity/water)
- Field agent site visits

## 6. Supporter / Funder / Partner Questions

### Q: What is a Supporter?
**A:** A Supporter (also called Funder or Partner) is an individual or entity who contributes capital to the Welile Rent Management Pool. Their money is used to pay landlords on behalf of tenants. In return, supporters earn monthly rewards on their invested capital.

### Q: How much can I earn as a supporter?
**A:** Supporters earn monthly rewards on their invested capital:

| Plan | Monthly ROI | Who Qualifies |
|------|-------------|---------------|
| Standard | 15% | Default for all new investments |
| Premium | 20% | Larger/longer commitments (configured per portfolio) |

**Example (Standard 15%, UGX 1,000,000, compounding over 12 months):**
- Month 1: UGX 150,000
- Month 6: UGX 301,704
- Month 12: UGX 651,895
- **Total earnings: ~UGX 3,997,860**

### Q: How do I invest?
**A:** There are three ways:
1. **Direct investment** — Fund your wallet via Mobile Money, then click "Fund Pool" from your Supporter Dashboard.
2. **Agent-facilitated** — An agent invests on your behalf from their wallet. Your portfolio is instantly activated.
3. **COO-initiated** — The operations team creates a portfolio on your behalf.

### Q: What is the minimum investment?
**A:** 
- **Direct pool funding:** Any amount greater than UGX 0
- **Agent proxy investment:** Minimum UGX 50,000

### Q: When do I receive my returns?
**A:** Returns follow one of two schedules:
- **Default:** 30-day cycle from your investment date (e.g., invest March 12 → first payout April 11)
- **Fixed calendar day:** The COO can set a fixed payout day (e.g., 15th of every month)

### Q: Can I withdraw my capital?
**A:** Yes, but with a **90-day notice period**:
1. Submit a withdrawal request
2. Your rewards are **paused** during the notice period
3. After 90 days, your capital is settled and returned
4. This protects the pool's liquidity and other supporters

### Q: What is the investment term?
**A:** The standard participation term is **12 months**. Capital withdrawal before maturity requires the 90-day notice process.

### Q: Can I see who my money is funding?
**A:** For privacy and security, supporters see **Virtual Houses** — anonymized representations of funded rent deals. You can see:
- Rent amounts and payment health indicators
- Repayment progress and risk status
- Financial outcomes (ROI earned)

You will **never** see tenant names, phone numbers, landlord details, or agent information.

### Q: Is my investment protected?
**A:** Welile employs multiple protection layers:
- **GPS verification** of all properties before funding
- **Tenant rights transfer** — Welile holds legal tenant rights during the facilitation period
- **15% solvency reserve** maintained by the platform
- **Double-entry ledger** ensuring complete financial audit trail
- **Field enforcement** capability for defaults

### Q: What is the ROI Calculator?
**A:** The ROI Calculator is a tool on your Supporter Dashboard that shows projected earnings based on your investment amount, chosen plan (15% or 20%), and duration. It displays a month-by-month compounding projection table.

## 7. Angel Pool & Equity Questions

### Q: What is the Angel Pool?
**A:** The Early Angel Pool is a capped equity structure allowing early supporters to own a share of Welile Technologies. Key parameters:
- **Total pool:** UGX 500,000,000
- **Total shares:** 25,000
- **Price per share:** UGX 20,000
- **Total equity represented:** 8% of company ownership

### Q: How does Angel Pool ownership work?
**A:** Ownership is calculated as:
- **Pool ownership %** = (your shares / 25,000) × 100
- **Company ownership %** = (your shares / 25,000) × 8

**Example:** If you hold 500 shares:
- Pool ownership: 2%
- Company ownership: 0.16%

### Q: Will I receive dividends from the Angel Pool?
**A:** The company is in a reinvestment phase. There is a **4-year dividend moratorium** — no dividends are expected for a minimum of four years. This is to prioritize platform growth and expansion.

### Q: Do Angel Pool participants have voting rights?
**A:** No. Angel Pool participants hold non-voting equity with no board representation and no management control. This is a passive investment structure.

### Q: Can I track my Angel Pool shares?
**A:** Yes. Your shares, investment history, and ownership percentages are visible in the "Angel Shares" tab within the "My Support Accounts" section of your Supporter Dashboard.

### Q: What happens if the company restructures?
**A:** Angel Pool participation may be converted into shares of Welile Technologies Inc as part of future global restructuring. All participants will be notified of any structural changes.

### Q: Does the Angel Pool investment replace previous agreements?
**A:** Yes. The current Angel Pool Agreement fully replaces all prior agreements with early contributors. All past contributions are consolidated into the current pool structure.

## 8. Payments, Wallets & Transactions

### Q: What payment methods are accepted?
**A:** Welile supports:
- **MTN Mobile Money**
- **Airtel Money**
- Other approved payment channels as they become available

### Q: How does my wallet work?
**A:** Every user has one wallet with a single balance in UGX. Money flows in through deposits and rewards, and flows out through investments, rent repayment, and withdrawals. Wallet balances are **never edited directly** — all changes go through the secure ledger system.

### Q: How do I deposit money into my wallet?
**A:** Deposit via Mobile Money (MTN or Airtel). The deposit is verified and credited to your Welile wallet. You can also receive deposits through agent-facilitated transactions.

### Q: How do I withdraw money?
**A:** Submit a withdrawal request from your dashboard. Withdrawals are processed through Mobile Money after approval by the Financial Operations team. **Minimum withdrawal: UGX 500.**

### Q: Are my transactions recorded?
**A:** Yes. Every transaction is recorded in a double-entry general ledger system. You can view your complete transaction history from your dashboard, including deposits, withdrawals, rewards, investments, and repayments.

### Q: What are push notifications for transactions?
**A:** You receive instant push notifications for all successful transactions, including:
- Wallet deposits
- Rent repayments
- Investment confirmations
- Reward payouts
- Withdrawal approvals

### Q: What is a transaction reference ID?
**A:** Every transaction gets a unique reference code for tracking. For example:
- Rent funding: `WRF260312-4827`
- Portfolio: `WPF-7291`

Keep these reference numbers for your records and any support inquiries.

## 9. Security & Privacy

### Q: How is my data protected?
**A:** Welile implements multiple security measures:
- **Row-Level Security (RLS)** — Users can only access their own data
- **Role-based access control** — Each dashboard shows only role-relevant information
- **Encrypted authentication** — Secure login with email verification
- **GPS verification** — All property and field activities are location-verified
- **Audit logging** — Every system action is logged for accountability

### Q: Can other users see my personal information?
**A:** No. The platform is designed with strict data isolation:
- Tenants cannot see other tenants' data
- Supporters cannot see tenant or landlord personal details
- Agents can only see their own assigned tenants
- Virtual Houses anonymize all identifiable information

### Q: Is my financial data safe?
**A:** Yes. Financial integrity is maintained through:
- **Single-Writer Principle** — Only the backend ledger system can modify wallet balances
- **No direct wallet edits** — Client-side code cannot modify wallets
- **Transaction groups** — All related financial operations are processed atomically
- **Complete audit trail** — Every financial change is permanently recorded

### Q: Who can access my account?
**A:** Only you can access your account with your credentials. Managers and operations staff can view certain operational data (like transaction status) but cannot modify your wallet balance or personal credentials. All admin actions are audit-logged.

**A:** Welile works best on modern browsers including:

## 11. Fees & Charges

### Q: What are all the fees on the platform?

| Fee | Amount | Who Pays | When |
|-----|--------|----------|------|
| Access Fee | 23%, 28%, or 33% (compounding monthly) | Tenant | On rent facilitation |
| Request Fee | UGX 10,000 or 20,000 | Tenant | Per rent request |
| Agent Advance Fee | 33% compounding | Agent | On cash advance |
| Agent Registration Fee | UGX 10,000 or 20,000 | Agent | Per advance |
| Landlord Management Fee | 2% of rent | Deducted from rent | When agent manages a non-smartphone landlord |

### Q: Are there hidden fees?
**A:** No. All fees are transparently displayed before any transaction is confirmed. You always see the full breakdown (rent + access fee + request fee = total repayment) before accepting.

### Q: Why do different rates exist (23%, 28%, 33%)?
**A:** The rates reflect different risk and duration profiles:
- **23% (Low)** — For shorter durations and returning tenants with good track records
- **28% (Medium)** — Standard facilitation rate
- **33% (High)** — For higher-risk situations or longer durations

### Q: Is there a fee for withdrawals?
**A:** Withdrawal processing may include standard Mobile Money charges. Withdrawals below UGX 500 are not processed.

## 12. Disputes, Defaults & Enforcement

### Q: What happens if a tenant defaults on repayment?
**A:** Welile follows a progressive enforcement process:
1. **SMS/Phone reminders** — Automated payment reminders
2. **Warnings** — Formal default notices
3. **Field agent visits** — Agents visit to collect or assess the situation
4. **Landlord & local leader cooperation** — Working with community structures
5. **Tenant replacement** — As a last resort, Welile may lawfully replace the tenant using its tenant rights

### Q: Can I dispute a transaction?
**A:** Yes. Contact Welile support at info@welile.com or call +256 744475573 / +256 764379713. All disputes are reviewed with full transaction audit trail available.

### Q: What if my landlord denies receiving payment?
**A:** Every landlord payment is tracked with:
- GPS-verified delivery confirmation
- Receipt photos uploaded by the agent
- Mobile Money transaction IDs
- Landlord signature (where applicable)
This documentation serves as proof of payment in any dispute.

### Q: What if I provide false information?
**A:** Per the Tenant Agreement, providing false information, blocking verification or enforcement, or bypassing platform payment channels is a violation of your agreement. This may result in account suspension, accelerated repayment demands, or legal action.

### Q: What is the "tenant rights transfer"?
**A:** When Welile pays rent to a landlord on your behalf, Welile temporarily holds the tenant rights for the paid rent period. You remain in the house as a house user/guest under Welile's tenant rights. This allows Welile to coordinate enforcement and replacement if you default. Your rights are fully restored once your repayment is complete.
