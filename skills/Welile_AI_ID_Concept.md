# Welile AI ID — Concept Overview

## What Is a Welile AI ID?

A **Welile AI ID** is a unique digital identity assigned to every user on the Welile platform. Think of it as a **financial fingerprint** — a short code (e.g. `WEL-A3F2B1`) that represents your trust and payment history without revealing your personal details.

It is generated automatically from your account and requires no extra sign-up or paperwork.

---

## Why Does It Exist?

In traditional rental markets, there is no easy way to prove you are a reliable tenant or a trustworthy landlord. Welile AI ID solves this by building a **living trust profile** behind a simple, shareable code.

### For Tenants
- Proves you pay rent on time.
- Unlocks higher borrowing limits as your score improves.
- Portable — you can share your AI ID with any landlord or funder.

### For Landlords & Funders
- Instantly check a tenant's reliability before approving a request.
- See a clear risk tier (Excellent → High Risk) without digging through paperwork.
- Make faster, data-backed decisions.

### For Agents
- Quickly look up any tenant's standing using their AI ID.
- Prioritise collections based on risk level.

---

## How Is the AI ID Generated?

The AI ID is **derived from your user account** — it is not a random code stored in a separate database. This means:

- It is **deterministic**: the same user always gets the same AI ID.
- It is **non-identifying**: knowing someone's AI ID does not reveal their name, phone number, or personal information.
- It is **instant**: no waiting, no approval process.

**Format:** `WEL-` followed by 6 alphanumeric characters (e.g. `WEL-4D9E0A`).

---

## What Data Sits Behind an AI ID?

When someone looks up an AI ID, they see a **summary profile** containing:

| Field | What It Means |
|---|---|
| **Risk Level** | A tier from *Excellent* to *High Risk* (or *New Member* for fresh accounts) |
| **Risk Score** | A numeric score reflecting overall reliability |
| **On-Time Payment Rate** | Percentage of repayments made on schedule |
| **Total Rent Facilitated** | Total value of rent processed through the platform |
| **Total Rent Requests** | Number of rent assistance requests made |
| **Funded Requests** | How many of those requests were successfully funded |
| **Estimated Borrowing Limit** | How much the user can currently borrow |
| **Wallet Balance** | Current wallet funds (visible to the user only) |
| **Referral Count** | Number of people referred to the platform |
| **Member Since** | When the user joined Welile |
| **Can Lend** | Whether the user qualifies to lend funds to others |

---

## Risk Tiers Explained

| Tier | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 🟢 **Excellent** | Consistently on-time payments, strong history |
| 🟢 **Good** | Mostly on-time, minor delays |
| 🔵 **Standard** | Average payment behaviour |
| 🟡 **Caution** | Some missed or late payments |
| 🔴 **High Risk** | Frequent defaults or unresolved debt |
| ⚪ **New Member** | Not enough history to rate yet |

Risk tiers update automatically as new payment data comes in — there is no manual intervention.

---

## How Is It Used Day-to-Day?

1. **Tenant applies for rent assistance** → Their AI ID score is checked automatically during approval.
2. **Funder reviews a request** → They see the tenant's AI ID tier and decide whether to fund.
3. **Landlord onboards a new tenant** → They can request the tenant's AI ID to verify reliability.
4. **Agent visits a tenant** → They glance at the AI ID card to understand the tenant's standing before the conversation.
5. **Peer lending** → Users with strong AI IDs can lend to others, with the system enforcing limits based on both parties' scores.

---

## Privacy & Security

- **No personal data is exposed** through the AI ID lookup — only aggregated financial behaviour.
- The AI ID cannot be reverse-engineered to find someone's name or contact details.
- Users control when and with whom they share their AI ID.

---

## Summary

The Welile AI ID turns **invisible trust into a visible, portable, and actionable asset**. It rewards good behaviour, protects funders from risk, and gives every participant a fair, transparent way to prove their financial reliability — all through a single six-character code.
