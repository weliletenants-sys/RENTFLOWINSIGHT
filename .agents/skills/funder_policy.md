## 2. Eligibility & Onboarding

### 2.1 Who Can Become a Supporter

Any individual aged 18 years or above, or any registered entity, may apply to become a Welile Supporter. Supporters must:

- Possess a valid Ugandan National ID or Passport
- Have an active mobile money account (MTN or Airtel Uganda)
- Accept the Supporter Participation Agreement via the platform
- Complete any KYC verification requested by Welile

### 2.2 Onboarding Channels

| Channel | Process |
|---------|---------|
| **Agent-Invited** | An agent creates a supporter invite via the `create-supporter-invite` function. The system generates a unique activation token and sends login credentials. Default password: `Welile` + last 6 digits of phone + `!` (e.g., phone `0700405936` → password `Welile405936!`) |
| **Bulk Import** | Partners can be imported in bulk by Operations via the `import-partners` function. Each partner receives a profile, wallet (balance: 0), supporter role, and activation invite |

### 2.3 Mandatory Agreement Acceptance

Before participating in any funding activity, every Supporter **must** read and accept the 12-Month Supporter Participation Agreement. The platform enforces this through a locked overlay — all funding buttons and investment features are disabled until the agreement is accepted. Acceptance is timestamped and recorded in the database.

## 3. Supporter Participation Agreement

### 3.1 Purpose

This Agreement governs your participation as a Tenant Supporter on welile.com, where you support verified tenant rent requests by funding rent for verified landlords and houses through Welile's platform processes. By clicking "I Agree", you confirm that you have read and accepted these Terms.

### 3.2 Welile's Nature and Role

Welile Technologies Limited is a technology company operating a platform (welile.com) that connects:
- Tenants seeking rent access
- Verified landlords and houses
- Tenant supporters who facilitate upfront rent payment
- Welile agents and managers who verify and enforce repayment

**Welile is not a bank, not a deposit-taking institution, and not an investment fund.** Welile operates as a verification, monitoring, enforcement, and tenant replacement coordination platform.

### 3.3 Contract Duration

- This Agreement runs for **twelve (12) months** from the Effective Date ("Contract Term").
- During the Contract Term, the Supporter may participate in multiple tenant support transactions through the platform.
- Unless renewed, this Agreement automatically expires at the end of 12 months.

### 3.4 Supporter Participation

- As a Supporter, you may select approved tenant rent requests displayed on your Supporter Dashboard.
- Each request includes landlord details, house verification, agent verification, manager approval, repayment period, and projected supporter outcome.
- Once you accept a request, rent is paid upfront for the tenant for a period of **30, 60, or 90 days** (as selected in the request).

### 3.5 Payment Flow and Custody of Funds

- Your support funds are used for the purpose of paying rent to verified landlords.
- Where applicable, rent payments and repayments may be processed and/or held by approved regulated payment partners.
- Welile's role is platform coordination, monitoring, and enforcement.

### 3.6 Repayment Structure

- Tenants repay through small daily instalments using the platform's approved payment channels.
- Tenant repayment includes principal rent amount, access fees, registration fees, and any other approved charges shown in the system.
- Repayments are monitored and enforced through Welile's agent network.

### 3.7 Supporter Responsibilities

Supporters agree to:
- Use the platform honestly and lawfully
- Avoid direct confrontation, harassment, or independent enforcement against tenants
- Follow dispute channels through Welile
- Comply with KYC/verification procedures if requested

## 4. Angel Pool Shareholders Agreement

### 4.1 Overview

The Company has established an **Early Angel Pool** — an equity participation structure that allows Supporters to acquire ownership stakes in Welile Technologies Limited.

### 4.2 Pool Structure

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Total Pool Cap** | UGX 500,000,000 |
| **Company Ownership** | Up to 8% of total company ownership |
| **Total Shares** | 25,000 |
| **Price Per Share** | UGX 20,000 |
| **Minimum Investment** | 1 share (UGX 20,000) |

### 4.3 Ownership Calculation

- **Pool Ownership %** = (Your Shares ÷ 25,000) × 100
- **Company Ownership %** = (Your Shares ÷ 25,000) × 8

**Example:** If you purchase 500 shares (UGX 10,000,000):
- Pool Ownership = (500 ÷ 25,000) × 100 = **2.00%**
- Company Ownership = (500 ÷ 25,000) × 8 = **0.16%**

### 4.4 Key Terms

1. **Supersession**: This Agreement fully replaces all prior agreements with early contributors. All past contributions are consolidated into this Angel Pool.
2. **Participation**: Participants do not hold direct shares but are part of the pooled structure. Allocation is proportional to contributions.
3. **Rights**: Participants have non-voting equity, no board representation, and no management control. This is a passive investment structure.
4. **Dividends**: The Company is in a reinvestment phase. No dividends are expected for a minimum of **four (4) years**.
5. **Future Structure**: Participation may be converted into Welile Technologies Inc as part of future global restructuring.
6. **Governance**: The Company retains full decision-making authority. Angel Pool participants have no governance rights.
7. **Risk**: This is a **high-risk, long-term investment with no guaranteed returns**.

### 4.5 Investment Process

1. Supporter navigates to Angel Pool tab on the dashboard
2. Must accept the Angel Pool Shareholders Agreement before investing
3. Enters investment amount (minimum UGX 20,000)
4. System validates wallet balance and pool capacity
5. On confirmation:
   - A `cash_out` entry is recorded in the general ledger
   - An `angel_pool_investments` record is created with status `confirmed`
   - Wallet balance is debited via the `sync_wallet_from_ledger` trigger
   - A unique reference ID is generated (format: `ANG[YYMMDD][4-digit-seq]`)

## 5. Wallet & Deposit Policy

### 5.1 Wallet Structure

Every Supporter receives a digital wallet upon registration. The wallet holds available funds that can be deployed to the Rent Management Pool or the Angel Pool.

### 5.2 Deposit Channels

| Channel | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **MTN Mobile Money** | Merchant Code: **090777** |
| **Airtel Money** | Merchant Code: **4380664** |
| **Bank Transfer** | Equity Bank Uganda (details provided on dashboard) |
| **Agent Cash** | Cash deposited via a Welile agent with receipt |

### 5.3 Deposit Process

1. Supporter makes payment via any channel above
2. Logs into welile.com and submits a deposit request with:
   - Transaction ID / Receipt number
   - Amount paid
   - Payment channel used
   - Optional: bank slip photo (uploaded to secure storage)
3. **Transaction ID Normalisation**: References are forced to uppercase and prefixed:
   - MoMo/Bank → prefixed with `TID`
   - Agent receipts → prefixed with `RCT`
4. **Time Window**: Submissions are restricted to a **7-day window**. Transactions older than 7 days or with future dates are rejected.

### 5.4 Verification

- **Auto-Approval**: If the submitted TID exists in `pre_registered_tids` (status: `waiting`, amount within UGX 1 tolerance), the deposit is instantly approved and the wallet is credited.
- **Manual Verification**: Otherwise, the deposit enters the manual verification queue where an operations manager reviews and approves or rejects it.

### 5.5 Wallet Integrity

- All wallet balance changes flow through the **General Ledger** exclusively.
- The `sync_wallet_from_ledger` database trigger recalculates balances atomically.
- No direct wallet balance edits are permitted — all changes must go through ledger entries.

## 7. Rent Management Pool Participation

### 7.1 How It Works

1. Tenants apply for rent access through the platform
2. Agents verify the tenant and landlord in person (GPS-verified)
3. Managers approve the rent request
4. The approved request appears as an "opportunity" on Supporter dashboards
5. A Supporter funds the request from their wallet
6. Welile pays the landlord on the tenant's behalf
7. The tenant repays in daily instalments over 30, 60, or 90 days

### 7.2 Pool Health Metrics

The dashboard displays pool health indicators:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Pool Balance** | Total capital available in the pool |
| **Total Deployed** | Capital currently out with tenants |
| **15% Reserve** | Locked solvency buffer (not deployable) |
| **Deployable** | Amount available for new rent requests |

### 7.3 Virtual Houses

For privacy protection, funded rent requests are displayed as **Virtual Houses** — anonymised representations that show:
- Rent amount and period
- Repayment progress
- Health status (Green / Amber / Red based on repayment behaviour)
- Agent and manager verification status

Supporters **never** see tenant personal details, addresses, or landlord contact information.

## 9. Withdrawal Policy

### 9.1 The 90-Day Notice Requirement

The Supporter may request withdrawal of supported capital by giving at least **ninety (90) days** written notice to Welile through official platform channels ("Withdrawal Notice").

### 9.2 During the Notice Period

- Welile coordinates collection of amounts due from active tenant repayment streams
- Repayment streams may be adjusted or prioritised for orderly settlement
- **Returns are paused** on capital under withdrawal notice

### 9.3 Settlement

At the end of the notice period, Welile will arrange payment to the Supporter as a **lump-sum settlement**, based on:
- Amounts collected during the notice period
- Enforcement, recovery, and replacement activities
- Platform reconciliation records

### 9.4 End-of-Contract Withdrawal

Where a Supporter provides withdrawal notice within the last 90 days of the Contract Term, Welile may treat the notice as a Contract Term settlement request. Settlement shall occur at Contract end or earlier where collection permits.

### 9.5 Approval Chain

Withdrawal requests go through a multi-step approval process:

```
Supporter submits request
    → Partner Operations reviews
        → COO provides operational clearance
            → CFO processes final payment
```

## 10. Principal & Outcome Assurance Framework

### 10.1 Framework Purpose

Welile provides a Principal and Outcome Assurance Framework designed to protect supporters against losses arising from tenant default.

### 10.2 Protection Mechanisms

| Mechanism | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Two-Layer Verification** | Agent (field) + Manager (operational) verification before any rent is paid |
| **Structured Repayments** | Daily micro-instalments reduce default risk |
| **Field Enforcement** | Agents visit tenants for collection and compliance monitoring |
| **Tenant Replacement** | Non-paying tenants can be replaced via the tenant rights transfer mechanism |
| **15% Reserve Lock** | A solvency buffer is maintained in the pool at all times |
| **GPS Verification** | All agent visits, collections, and landlord payments are GPS-stamped |

### 10.3 Legal Clarification

- Supporter participation is **not** presented as a deposit, savings product, or regulated security
- Welile does **not** offer an interest-bearing account
- Welile provides platform-based repayment continuity and loss resolution through enforcement and replacement
- This assurance is achieved **operationally** — not as a financial guarantee

## 11. Tenant Rights Transfer & Enforcement

### 11.1 How It Works

- The tenant accepts the transfer of tenant rights to Welile at the time they apply for rent through the platform
- When rent is paid to the landlord, Welile becomes the **holder of tenant rights** for the paid rent period
- The physical tenant becomes a **house user** under Welile's tenancy rights

### 11.2 Enforcement Powers

This mechanism enables Welile to:
- Coordinate lawful enforcement actions
- Execute tenant replacement when necessary
- Maintain repayment continuity
- Collaborate with landlords and local leadership structures (including LC1 Chairpersons)

### 11.3 Supporter Protection

Supporters are not required to take any enforcement action. All enforcement is handled by Welile's agent and management network. Supporters must **never** directly confront, threaten, or communicate with tenants about repayments.

Welile may suspend or terminate supporter participation in cases of:

- Identity misrepresentation
- Abuse of tenants (verbal, physical, or financial)
- Platform circumvention

### 14.2 Investigation Process

- Welile will notify the Supporter of the investigation
- Account access may be restricted during investigation
- The Supporter will be given opportunity to respond

### 14.3 Asset Recovery During Suspension

- Deployed capital continues to be managed by the platform during suspension
- Returns may be paused pending investigation outcome
- Upon clearance, normal operations resume
- Upon termination, the 90-day withdrawal process is initiated
